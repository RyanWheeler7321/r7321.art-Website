## Snapshot

*Tears Fall Low* is a 2D precision platformer with a short emotional story. Compared to earlier work, it reads as a more focused project with a clearer mechanical identity.

- Precision-platforming as the main skill test.
- Shorter, more concentrated structure.
- Emotional framing instead of a purely systems-first pitch.

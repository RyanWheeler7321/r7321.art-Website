## Snapshot

*Sidereal* is a 3D world-exploration game built around one hundred distinct worlds, each with its own visuals, music, and story context.

## Scope

The project was intentionally huge. That came with the usual costs: scene count, content maintenance, platform concerns, and a constant need to decide what was actually worth finishing. It still worked as the right kind of project to learn from because it forced a lot of production problems into the open.

## What carried forward

- Larger-scale content organization.
- Shipping a visually broader 3D game.
- Harder lessons about scope, finish lines, and where quality actually comes from.

## Snapshot

*Sunrise 7* was the first released game in the catalog. It is a 2D platformer adventure with procedurally generated levels, a deliberate difficulty spike, and an early version of the tone that would keep showing up in later work.

## What stands out

- First shipped title.
- Procedural level generation built into a story-driven structure.
- Clear emphasis on challenge and atmosphere.
